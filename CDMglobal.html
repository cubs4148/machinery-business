About Us

Established in 1996, CDMglobal has been a trusted leader in the second-hand machinery business for over two decades. With offices in Chicago, Illinois, Lima, Peru, New Delhi, India, and Ahmedabad, Gujarat, we proudly serve clients across the globe.

We specialize in high-quality metalworking machinery, including CNC machines, EDMs, lathes, and a variety of other industrial equipment. Our mission is to provide reliable, cost-effective solutions for businesses of all sizes, from small workshops to large-scale manufacturing units. Each machine we offer is thoroughly inspected and meets stringent industry standards to ensure maximum performance and longevity.

With our deep expertise and commitment to excellence, we aim to help our clients enhance their production capabilities and achieve operational efficiency. Whether you're upgrading your existing equipment or starting a new venture, we are your go-to partner for premium second-hand machinery.

Led by Chirag Chaudhari, a professional with a strong background in business analysis and data intelligence, our team is dedicated to delivering unparalleled customer service and tailored solutions.
