About Us

Established in 1996, CDMglobal has been a trusted leader in the second-hand machinery business for over two decades. With offices in Chicago, Illinois, Lima, Peru, New Delhi, India, and Ahmedabad, Gujarat, we proudly serve clients across the globe.

We specialize in high-quality metalworking machinery, including CNC machines, EDMs, lathes, and a variety of other industrial equipment. Our mission is to provide reliable, cost-effective solutions for businesses of all sizes.
