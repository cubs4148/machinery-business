Who We Are

NxT Machinery is a trusted supplier of high-quality second-hand CNC, EDM, and metalworking machines. We help manufacturers worldwide access reliable machinery at competitive prices, without the risk and cost of buying new equipment.

I’m Chirag Chaudhari, founder of NxT Machinery. With years of experience in business analysis, operations, and industrial equipment sourcing, I started NxT Machinery with one clear goal — to make high-quality machinery accessible to growing manufacturers worldwide.

We focus on transparency, technical understanding, and long-term relationships. Every machine we deal with is evaluated carefully to ensure it delivers real value to our clients.
