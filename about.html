About Us

Empowering industries with reliable and high-performance machinery since 1996.

Hello, I'm Chirag Chaudhari, the founder of NxT Machinery. With a background in business analysis and data intelligence, I bring over 20 years of experience in the second-hand machinery industry. My passion for optimizing production processes and providing high-quality equipment has been the foundation of NxT Machinery's growth.

Our Vision

Our vision is to deliver cost-effective solutions to industries worldwide, ensuring the reliability and high performance of all machinery offerings, while supporting our clients in enhancing their operational efficiency and scaling their businesses.

1996 - Company Founded

NxT Machinery was established in Chicago, Illinois, aiming to provide affordable, high-quality industrial machinery.

2000 - Expansion to South America

Opened an office in Lima, Peru to better serve the growing market in South America.

2010 - Indian Market Entry

NxT Machinery expanded to New Delhi and Ahmedabad, strengthening our presence in the Asian market.
